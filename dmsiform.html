DMS Assessment Form

DMSI Assessment Instructions

  • No right or wrong answers. Go with your initial instinct.
  • For each question, assign scores:
    • 8 = most like you
    • 4 = second most like you
    • 2 = third most like you
    • 1 = least like you
  • Use each score (8, 4, 2, 1) only once per question.
  • Reflect your true self, not what you think is desirable.
  • Consider typical work decisions when responding.
  • Total the columns after completing all questions.

Example: Q1: 8 for "Recognition", 4 for "Status", 2 for "Security", 1 for "Excellence".

Source: Rowe, A. J., & Mason, O. R. (1987). Managing with style: A guide to understanding, assessing, and improving decision making. San Francisco, CA: Jossey-Bass.
